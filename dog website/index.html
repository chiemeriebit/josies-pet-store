The Dogs

Poouler Do breeds

This is a list of popular dog breeds in Nigeria with some key information about them

Lhasa Apso

Temperament Lhasa Apsos are intelligent, independent, and playful dogs. They are affectionate with their owners but can be wary of strangers.

Life Span 12-15 years

Grooming Lhasa Apsos have a long, flowing coat that requires daily brushing to prevent matting and tangles. Regular grooming appointments are also recommended.

Health Issues Lhasa Apsos are prone to respiratory problems, allergies, and hip dysplasia.

German Shepherd

Temperament German Shepherds are intelligent, loyal, and protective dogs. They are great with families and make excellent working dogs.

Life Span 9-13 years

Grooming German Shepherds have a dense double coat that sheds heavily twice a year. Regular brushing is recommended to keep their coat healthy and shiny.

Health Issues German Shepherds are prone to hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, and degenerative myelopathy.

Bull Mastiff

Temperament Bull Mastiffs are calm, loyal, and protective dogs. They are great with families but can be reserved with strangers.

Life Span 8-10 years

Grooming Bull Mastiffs have a short, dense coat that is easy to care for. Occasional brushing is all that is needed to keep their coat in good condition.

Health Issues Caucasian Mastiffs are prone to hip dysplasia, heart disease, and skin allergies.

Caucasian Mastiff

Temperament Caucasian Mastiffs are confident, courageous, and independent dogs. They are loyal to their owners but can be aggressive with strangers.

Life Span 10-12 years

Grooming Caucasian Mastiffs have a thick, dense coat that requires regular brushing to prevent matting and tangles. They also need occasional baths to keep their coat clean.

Health Issues Lhasa Apsos are prone to respiratory problems, allergies, and hip dysplasia.

Boerboel

Temperament Boerboels are intelligent, confident, and protective dogs. They are great with families but can be wary of strangers.

Life Span 10-12 years

Grooming Boerboels have a short, dense coat that is easy to care for. Occasional brushing is all that is needed to keep their coat in good condition.

Health Issues Boerboels are prone to hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, and bloat.

Rottweiler

Temperament Rottweilers are loyal, confident, and protective dogs. They are great with families but can be reserved with strangers.

Life Span 8-10 years

Grooming Rottweilers have a short, dense coat that requires minimal grooming. Regular brushing is all that is needed to keep their coat in good condition.

Health Issues Rottweilers are prone to hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, and bloat. They are also at risk for cancer and eye problems.
